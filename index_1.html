Connecting Kenya

Our belief is that everyone deserves access to connectivity.

Building Our digital future together

ATTC Connect Ltd is a dynamic Kenyan tech start-up that was founded and registered in October 2018 by Fred Macha, Martin Njau, and George Chomba Kariuki. In January 2020, the Communication Authority (CA) granted the company the NFP-T3 (TL/NFP/T3/00769) & ASP (TL/ASP/00769) licenses, enabling it to conduct internet connectivity business. Our company specializes in providing high-quality internet services to residents, homeowners, and businesses in Nairobi, Kiambu, and their immediate surroundings. With a long-term vision of exponential growth, our ultimate goal is to extend our services to the broader Kenyan market.

 

 

OUR MISSION

To Provide high quality connectivity to as many Kenyans as possible supporting them and their businesses and homes achieve an ever growing competitive edge in our fast growing digital economy.
